Precision Engineering

WHY EXACTING STANDARDS MATTER

Modern vehicles are engineered with highly complex crumple zones designed to absorb impact and protect passengers. When a severe collision occurs, this underlying structure is heavily compromised, even if the exterior panels don't look destroyed.

At Northstar Auto in Laval, we strictly utilize manufacturer-approved repair protocols. We don't just hammer out dents and apply filler; we replace damaged high-strength steel, re-weld structural joints with precision, and ensure your vehicle meets strict OEM safety standards before it ever hits the paint booth.

OUR BODYWORK SERVICES

Major Collision Reconstruction

Severe impacts require more than cosmetic fixes. Our facility is equipped to handle heavy hits, OEM panel replacements, and full front-end reconstructions. We carefully tear down the damaged area to expose hidden structural bends, meticulously mapping out the necessary repairs to restore integrity.

  • Authentic OEM Panel Replacement
  • Advanced High-Strength Steel Welding

Laser Frame Straightening

A bent frame compromises the drivability and safety of your vehicle. Using state-of-the-art computerized laser measuring systems, we anchor your car and apply precise hydraulic pressure to pull the chassis back to exact manufacturer specifications, down to the millimeter.

  • 3D Computerized Diagnostics
  • Restores Perfect Wheel Alignment

Paintless Dent Repair (PDR)

For minor dents, door dings, and hail damage where the factory paint remains perfectly intact, our PDR specialists massage the metal from behind the panel. This highly skilled technique restores the shape instantly without the need for fillers, sanding, or repainting.

  • Cost-Effective & Fast Turnaround
  • Retains Original Factory Paint

Rust, Aluminum & ADAS

Quebec winters are incredibly harsh on metal. We cut out deep corrosion and weld in fresh steel. Furthermore, we maintain specialized isolated bays for complex Aluminum body repairs (like the Ford F-150) and perform complete ADAS safety sensor recalibrations post-repair.

  • Specialized Aluminum Tooling
  • Subaru EyeSight & Sensor Calibration
