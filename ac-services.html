WHY A/C MATTERS AFTER AN ACCIDENT

The most vulnerable part of your vehicle's air conditioning system is the condenser. Located directly behind your front grille, it is almost always damaged during front-end collisions.

When the condenser is pierced, the system loses its refrigerant and pressure. At Northstar Auto, we fully replace damaged A/C components, vacuum test the lines, and recharge the system in-house so you leave with a fully restored vehicle.

UV Leak Detection

A/C systems are closed; low freon means a leak. We inject UV dye into the lines to pinpoint microscopic leaks in hoses, seals, or the evaporator.
