Structural Restoration

EXPERT
BODYWORK

At Northstar Auto, our bodywork goes far beyond surface-level aesthetics. We meticulously restore your vehicle's structural integrity, ensuring it meets strict factory safety standards after an accident.

Collision & Dent Repair

From minor door dings to major collision reconstructions, our certified technicians expertly massage out dents, align panels flawlessly, and eliminate all traces of impact.

Laser Frame Straightening

We utilize advanced computerized laser measuring systems to pull and straighten your vehicle's chassis back to its exact manufacturer specifications, down to the millimeter.

Rust Remediation & Welding

We cut out corrosion and weld in fresh metal. Our team handles specialized materials, including high-strength steel and aluminum panels, ensuring long-lasting durability.

Flawless Finishes

EXPERT PAINT &
REFINISHING

Our refinishing process guarantees a showroom-quality finish. We don't just spray paint; we engineer a durable, vibrant coat that protects your vehicle and turns heads on the road.

Computerized Color Matching

Sunlight and time alter your car's color. We use cutting-edge spectrophotometers to read your vehicle's current pigment, mixing an exact micro-match to blend new paint seamlessly with the old.

Downdraft Bake Booths

Our painting is performed in climate-controlled, pressurized downdraft booths. This eliminates dust and impurities, while the high-heat baking process cures the paint for a factory-hardened, mirror-like shine.

Clear Coat & Scratch Repair

From localized scratch removal to full custom resprays, we apply premium, eco-friendly waterborne basecoats followed by high-solid clear coats to provide ultimate UV and scratch resistance.

Climate Control

A/C REPAIR &
RECHARGE

Don't lose your cool. Whether it's a routine freon recharge, leak detection, or fixing a condenser pierced in a front-end collision, we restore your vehicle's climate control to factory standards.

Evacuation & Recharge

We safely recover old refrigerant, vacuum the system to remove moisture, and recharge it using modern R-134a or R-1234yf refrigerants.

Advanced Leak Detection

A/C systems are closed; low freon means a leak. We use specialized UV dye and electronic sniffers to pinpoint and repair microscopic leaks.

Collision A/C Repair

Front-end impacts almost always damage the condenser. We handle complete replacements and compressor repairs in-house.
